Tila

Meet our master hair stylist and owner of Tila hair studio, Tila. Tila started her passion in this exciting and trendy industry 15 years ago whilst working at Toni & Guy where she originally trained. Being one of the most prestigious salons in the world, Tila's talent grew rapidly making her the hair guru she is today.

With a desire to be successful, Tila opened her own salon in West Hampstead in 2014 and ever since has attracted a fantastic clientele both local and international. A wonderful eye for detail ensures your colour is perfect every time you leave the salon.

Tila specialises in colour, cut, up styles and Extensions. We ensure you will always leave looking beautiful. And don't worry, whether your hair is fine, straight or curly, her techniques are mastered for all!

Adela

Adela joined Tila Studio in 2016 and has attracted a vibrant and loyal clientele both locally and nationally.

Another string to her bow was her recent completion of her master colour course, bringing you the latest trends in colour.

Whether it's styling, blowdrys, cut or colour you're after, you are in really talented hands. Adela is known to always exceed clients expectations with classic skill and modern techniques. From balayage to highlights or crazy colours, her eye for detail will leave you looking glam.
