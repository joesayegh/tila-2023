TILA STUDIO is an exciting, independent hair and make-up salon that first opened in December 2014. We’re located in West Hampstead, Fortune Green Road, London NW6.

We provide a warm and friendly atmosphere offering bespoke services using only the best products on the market and first class customer service. We specialise in Cutting, Colouring, Balayage, Olaplex, Keratin treatment, Make-up, Wedding Packages and Hair ups that are tailored to your individual needs. We offer premium haircare products such as Goldwell, Kerasilk, KMS, Olaplex and Morrocan Oil. We support hair care brands that we consider to be the best in the business.

Tila

Meet our master hair stylist and owner of Tila hair studio, Tila. Tila started her passion in this exciting and trendy industry 15 years ago whilst working at Toni & Guy where she originally trained. Being one of the most prestigious salons in the world, Tila's talent grew rapidly making her the hair guru she is today.

With a desire to be successful, Tila opened her own salon in West Hampstead in 2014 and ever since has attracted a fantastic clientele both local and international. A wonderful eye for detail ensures your colour is perfect every time you leave the salon.

Tila specialises in colour, cut, up styles and Extensions. We ensure you will always leave looking beautiful. And don't worry, whether your hair is fine, straight or curly, her techniques are mastered for all!

Adela

Adela joined Tila Studio in 2016 and has attracted a vibrant and loyal clientele both locally and nationally.

Another string to her bow was her recent completion of her master colour course, bringing you the latest trends in colour.

Whether it's styling, blowdrys, cut or colour you're after, you are in really talented hands. Adela is known to always exceed clients expectations with classic skill and modern techniques. From balayage to highlights or crazy colours, her eye for detail will leave you looking glam.

Brikena

Before joining Tila Studio in January 2024, Brikena worked in a number of other prestigious salons in London including Hershesons, gaining over 10 years of experience in cut and colour.

With a focus on all-gender hairstyles, Brikena brings a wealth of experience and a keen eye for detail to every appointment. Her personalised approach and commitment to staying updated on the latest trends ensure that each client receives a tailored and exceptional service.

Step into Brikena's chair at Tila Studio and let her expertise transform your look. With her seamless blend of creativity and precision, Brikena is dedicated to delivering outstanding results that exceed expectations.

Deposit Policy

We request a deposit of £25 from every guest booking a colour and cut service. This deposit will be used as part payment towards your service. If you fail to show up for your appointment, we reserve the right to retain your deposit in line with our cancellation policy.

Cancellation Policy

We require 48 hours’ notice of cancellation for all our guests. If appointments are cancelled with less than 48 hours’ notice, then unfortunately, your deposit will be non-refundable. We completely understand that life’s little dramas can get in the way of scheduled appointments, and we can assure you that we will be sympathetic in certain circumstances.

Regrettably, we have had to put these measures in place due to increasing numbers of no-shows, and late cancellations, which is costing the salon hundreds of pounds in lost revenue each month.

We would like to thank you for your understanding and co-operation.
